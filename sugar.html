Residual Sugar

the more residual sugar in the wine, the sweeter tasting it is. Wines that tended to have a larger residual sugar level, were rated in the middle, 4, 5, 6, 7 quality levels. The higher quality wine had the lowest residual sugar, therefore a more tart flavor compared to the other lower rated wines.
